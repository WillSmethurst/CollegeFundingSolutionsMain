Who We Are

An Advisor-Led Firm Built Around the Full College Journey

Strategy West College Planning is an advisor-led college planning firm that works with families from 9th grade through enrollment. We help parents and students navigate the college process with more structure, more clarity, and more confidence than they would have going through it on their own.

Most families approach this process reactively, responding to deadlines, guessing at financial aid, and making major decisions without a clear framework in place. We exist to change that.

We work with families across the country, guiding them through every dimension of college planning: academic positioning, student development, and financial strategy.

Our Promise

What We Are Here to Do

We will be a clear, honest, and proactive guide through one of the most complex processes your family will face.

We will not tell you what you want to hear. We will tell you what you need to know, early enough to act on it.

Our Mission

What We Are Working Toward, Together

For parents

To reduce the uncertainty and stress of the college planning process and replace it with a clear, step-by-step strategy built around your family's specific situation.

For students

To align academic preparation, personal development, and college selection so they arrive at the right school ready to thrive.

For families

To make informed, confident decisions about where to send your student and how to fund it responsibly.
