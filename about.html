Who We Are

An Advisor-Led Firm Built Around the Full College Journey

College Funding Solutions is a college planning advisory firm that works with families from 9th grade through enrollment day. We help parents and students navigate the college process with more structure, more clarity, and more confidence than they would have going through it on their own.

We built this firm because the college planning process is genuinely more complex than most families realize — and because the consequences of poor planning are real, lasting, and largely avoidable with the right guidance in place.

The college process is not just about where your student applies. It is about how they position themselves academically. It is about how they develop as a person and what story they tell. It is about understanding how financial aid actually works — and making informed decisions before the deadline closes every option you didn't know you had.

"We treat college planning as what it actually is: a multi-year, multi-dimensional challenge that rewards families who approach it with intention."

Academic Preparation

Course selection, GPA trajectory, standardized testing strategy, and building an academic record that opens doors — not one that quietly closes them.

Student Development

Activities, leadership, community engagement, and personal narrative — developing a student profile that communicates character, not just achievement.

Financial Strategy

FAFSA, CSS Profile, merit aid positioning, award letter analysis, and helping families understand what college actually costs — before they commit to it.

Our Approach

What Makes Our Approach Different

Most college planning resources address one piece of the process in isolation. We address all three — simultaneously and in coordination — because that is how the college process actually works.

Pillar One

Academic Guidance

The academic record your student builds between 9th and 12th grade is the foundation of both admissions competitiveness and scholarship eligibility. We help families understand what strong positioning looks like — and how to build it with intention.

Course rigor and GPA management by grade year
Test strategy, timing, and score positioning
School list development and admissions fit analysis
Pillar Two

Social Fit & Student Readiness

Admissions decisions are about who your student is, not just what they have achieved. We help families develop an authentic, coherent student narrative that translates naturally into compelling applications and a strong sense of personal direction.

Extracurricular depth and leadership development
Personal narrative and application story
Campus culture fit and long-term student readiness
Pillar Three

Financial Strategy & Cost Clarity

Understanding how financial aid and merit scholarships actually work — and positioning your family accordingly — can meaningfully change what college costs. We help families navigate this process with clarity, not guesswork.

FAFSA and CSS Profile guidance and preparation
Merit aid positioning and school selection strategy
Award letter analysis and enrollment decision support
Why Families Work With Us

Structure and Clarity Where the Process Offers Neither

The college planning process is full of information — and most of it is contradictory, incomplete, or not tailored to your student's actual situation. Families come to us not because they haven't tried to figure it out, but because the volume and complexity of what they're trying to figure out has exceeded what they can do alone.

What we offer is not more information. It is a structured, advisor-led process that turns the complexity of college planning into a clear, sequenced plan — built around your family's goals, your student's profile, and the financial reality you are working within.

Families who work with us from 9th grade tend to arrive at enrollment day with more options, better outcomes, and a clear understanding of the decisions they made and why. Families who come to us later still benefit — there is meaningful work we can do at every stage.

Our Philosophy

Guidance That Is Strategic,
Personalized, and Honest

The college process is one of the most consequential planning exercises a family will undertake. We approach it accordingly — with the seriousness, depth, and care it deserves.

Belief One

Every Family's Situation Is Different

There is no one-size-fits-all path through the college process. A strategy that works well for one family may not serve another. Effective guidance starts with listening — to your goals, your circumstances, and your student's genuine strengths and interests.

Belief Two

Thoughtful Planning Matters

The families who navigate this process most successfully are not the ones who work hardest at the end. They are the ones who planned deliberately at the beginning. Timing matters. Sequencing matters. Early decisions close or open doors that come later.

Belief Three

College Decisions Extend Well Beyond Four Years

Where a student attends college — and at what cost — shapes the financial and professional trajectory of their early adult life. These decisions deserve the same seriousness families bring to any major long-term investment. We help families make them with both clarity and confidence.

Belief Four

Honesty Is the Foundation of Good Guidance

No ethical advisor promises specific admissions results, scholarship amounts, or financial aid outcomes. We don't make those promises. What we do promise is honest, thorough guidance — built around what is actually possible for your family and your student.

"Our goal is not to take the process off your plate. It is to walk through it with you — so that every decision you make is an informed one, and every opportunity available to your family is one you actually know about."
